Welcome

'VOLUME 1' is an online digital edition of the first volume of Karl Marx’s Das Kapital. First published just over 150 years ago, Vol.1 of Marx’s Capital - A Critique of Political Economy on "The Process of Production of Capital" has endured as an important touchstone for scholars in across diverse fields in the humanities and social sciences, as well as for activists and intellectuals of all types with an interest in the experience and conditions of life under capitalism, whose radical critique of the human cost of modern industrial development that has had controversial and far-reaching effects in global politics and thought. 'VOLUME 1' brings together extant scholarly sources to create a multi-layered annotated edition of the text providing critical reading guides and supplemental historical and sociological data into one easily accessible and user-friendly interface. Additionally, Volume 1 delivers an interactive platform for creating and preserving readers own collaboratively produced annotated editions of the text for undergraduate classes, reading groups or graduate level seminars.
