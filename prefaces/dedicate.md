DEDICATED

TO MY UNFORGETTABLE FRIEND

Wilhelm Wolff

INTREPID, FAITHFUL,

NOBLE PROTAGONIST OF THE PROLETARIAT

Born in Tarnau on June 21, 1809

Died in exile in Manchester on May 9, 1864
